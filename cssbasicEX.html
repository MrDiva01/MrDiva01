UC HYMN

Mid these fair Visayan Isles 'Neath these azure blue skies Stands UC our beloved Lofty in all her glory

Sparks of wisdom, hope and joy Always kindling in our hearts Thy precious name, thy glorious fame Will shine through all eternity

Thy name shall ever be our guide Thy light our inspiration Keep up the light a glowing bright To thy portals dear ALMA MATER

Hail! Praises be to thee Onwards show us the way Faithful and true we'll always be To thee our ALMA MATER dear.
